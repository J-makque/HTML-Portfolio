"Godzilla" (2014), directed by Gareth Edwards - This version of the film stars Aaron Taylor-Johnson, Bryan Cranston, and Elizabeth Olsen and was released on May 16, 2014."Shin Godzilla" (2016), directed by Hideaki Anno and Shinji Higuchi - This Japanese film was released on July 29, 2016 and features a different take on the classic monster.

"Godzilla vs. Kong" (2021), directed by Adam Wingard - This crossover film between the Godzilla and King Kong franchises was released on March 31, 2021 and features an epic showdown between the two titans.

"The Godfather"

"The Godfather" (1972), directed by Francis Ford Coppola - This iconic crime drama stars Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, and James Caan and was released on March 24, 1972. Set in New York City in the 1940s, it tells the story of the Corleone family and their involvement in organized crime. With powerful performances, a gripping story, and unforgettable scenes, "The Godfather" is widely regarded as a masterpiece.

"Citizen Kane"

"Citizen Kane" (1941), directed by Orson Welles - Often considered the greatest film ever made, "Citizen Kane" was released on September 5, 1941. Starring Welles himself, the film tells the story of a wealthy newspaper magnate who dies, and a reporter who sets out to discover the meaning behind the enigmatic last word he spoke: "Rosebud." With innovative techniques and a complex narrative structure, "Citizen Kane" broke new ground in filmmaking and has influenced countless films that came after it.
